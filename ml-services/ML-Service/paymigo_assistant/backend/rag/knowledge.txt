# PayMigo Knowledge Base

## 1. What is PayMigo?
PayMigo is a financial co-pilot and income protection platform designed specifically for gig workers (delivery drivers, rideshare drivers, etc.). It helps workers stabilize their income by providing payouts during low-demand periods or unexpected disruptions.

## 2. Subscription Plans
- **PayMigo Lite**: $5/month. Payout cap: $100/month. Includes basic income tracking.
- **PayMigo Pro**: $15/month. Payout cap: $500/month. Includes demand forecasting and basic GeoTruth verification.
- **PayMigo Elite**: $25/month. Payout cap: $1,500/month. Includes full GeoTruth protection, priority support, and premium analytics.

## 3. Trigger Rules
Payouts are triggered based on:
- **Low Demand**: When regional gig demand falls 30% below the 3-month average.
- **Extreme Weather**: When weather conditions (heavy rain, snow, extreme heat) prevent safe working conditions.
- **Curfew/Lockdown**: Official government-mandated curfews in the worker's operational area.

## 4. Claim Workflow
1. **Initiation**: User opens the Migo assistant or "Claims" tab and selects "New Claim".
2. **Verification**: Migo checks the current triggers (weather/demand) and validates the user's location via GeoTruth.
3. **Approval**: If triggers are active and location is verified, the claim is approved instantly.
4. **Payout**: Funds are disbursed to the linked wallet within 24 hours.

## 5. GeoTruth Explanation
GeoTruth is PayMigo's proprietary location verification system. It cross-references GPS data with cell tower triangulation and known Wi-Fi hotspots to ensure a user is actually in the zone they claim to be. This prevents "spoofing" and ensures payouts only go to workers affected by local conditions.

## 6. Fraud and Spoofing
PayMigo has a zero-tolerance policy for GPS spoofing. If GeoTruth detects a mismatch between GPS and network location, the account is flagged for review. Repeated attempts result in a permanent ban.

## 7. Loyalty Rules
- **Gold Member**: 6 months of continuous subscription. 10% bonus on all claims.
- **Platinum Member**: 12 months+ of subscription. 20% bonus on all claims and 1 free "emergency payout" per year regardless of triggers.

## 8. Support and Escalation
- **Migo**: Handles basic questions and status checks.
- **Level 1 Support**: Human agents for claim disputes. Escalated if Migo cannot resolve the intent for 3 consecutive turns.
- **Level 2 Support**: Technical issues or fraud appeals.

## 9. Tone of Voice
Migo is helpful, professional, and empathetic. It uses clear, concise language and avoids overly technical jargon unless explaining GeoTruth. Migo should always try to be encouraging to gig workers.
